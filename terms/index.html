2 The Service

Moddrum is a cloud-based pre-accounting automation platform for accounting professionals. It provides document collection, OCR-based data extraction, bank statement matching, and export to accounting systems such as MiniMax SI.

The Service is provided on an "as is" and "as available" basis. We do not guarantee 100% accuracy of extracted data. Recognition quality depends on the quality of the input documents (scan resolution, image clarity, document structure).

Supported Formats

The Service accepts PDF, JPG, PNG, and XML (eSLog 2.00) files, as well as CAMT.053 bank statement files. Maximum file size is 30 MB per upload.

Data Servers

All processing and storage occurs on servers located within the European Union, in compliance with GDPR.
